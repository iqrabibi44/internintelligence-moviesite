Our Mission

At Filmagnet, we believe everyone deserves access to great cinema. Our mission is to break down barriers to film appreciation by providing an affordable, accessible platform that celebrates diverse voices in filmmaking.

We're committed to supporting independent filmmakers while bringing you the best of mainstream cinema, all in one place.
